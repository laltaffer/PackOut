We’ve designed this to be basic and a great starting point to plan the most basic to the most challenging of your backcountry hunts, hikes, and adventures.   These standards are created based off of performance fueling and standards we’ve seen work with most top tier athletes.  With that said, we strongly encourage three things:   (1) this is a starting point.  You may need more or less of a specific nutrient that we have listed.    (2) As much as you’re able, we encourage you to trial things while on the trail and in training.  Something may sound good in a grocery store isle, but repulsive after you’ve been covering country all day long.     (3) Finally, we encourage you to find your practical amidst an “optimal” calculation- this may mean modifying to fit your dietary preferences, food intolerances, allergies, etc.   Don’t be afraid to venture outside the boxes of our guidelines if you know it sits better with you. Breakfast doesn't need to have a specific calorie goal, but having some fuel can be helpful.  Minimum suggestion: 200-400kcal, 40-60g CHO.
